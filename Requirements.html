Troubleshooting "accounts.google.com refused to connect" message

Double-check that you are logged into Google (especially if you are logging in on a different computer, in incognito mode, etc.).

To check your settings, go to the Google Chrome Settings -> Privacy and Security -> Cookies and other site data

Ensure that your Chrome settings are not blocking "Third Party Cookies". You may set your settings to "Allow all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies in Incognito".
